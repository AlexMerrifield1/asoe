"Why not just use Gemini?" 
Gemini requires manual prompting every time. A gem has worked, but relevant context is still gathered manually. This is a system - Slack triggers it, n8n orchestrates it, outputs land in Drive automatically. It's repeatable, auditable, and doesn't require us to be prompt engineers every time.

"What's the ROI?" 
Current state: 30 min per personalized outreach, inconsistent quality. Future state: 5 minutes (form fill + review), consistent with our best frameworks. If we do 10 outreaches/week, that's 4 hours back to the team or the opportunity to do 5X the outreach. Plus, higher response rates from better personalization.

"What are the risks?" 
The biggest risk is prompt drift - AI outputs need monitoring. 
We solve this with: 
(1) Fact-check agent for validation, 
(2) Human review before sending, 
(3) Output templates to constrain formatting. 
It's AI-assisted, not AI-automated.

"Can we white-label this for clients?" 
Absolutely. Same system, different templates. We could offer this as an EaaSe add-on - 'AI-Powered Outreach Automation' for our clients' sales teams.

"What's the timeline?" 
Phase 1 (MVP): 2-3 weeks - Basic Slack → n8n → AI → Drive flow for one workflow type (Net New?). 
Phase 2: Add enrichment layer (Apollo, web scraping). 
Phase 3: All three workflow types + refinement.
