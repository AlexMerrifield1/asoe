# Fulfillment and Follow-Up (FF) Overview & Script

---

## PLAYS:

### 1. Reframe
- **Case Open/Needs Audit:** CO/NA *(We talked about…)*
- **Solution Presentation/Closing Interactions:** SP/CI *(We agreed that...)*

### 2. Fulfill Promises
- *Completed Expectations*
- *Had problems along the way and we solved them*

### 3. Bridge Statement → Collect Proof

### 4. Harvest Referrals

### 5. SNE

---

## SCRIPT FOR A FULL FF (SAMPLE):

### Reframe:

**(CO/NA)** When we first engaged, we talked about [Recap goals and objectives: ..."*your growth and how you had many manual processes that were bottlenecks in your organization. You also talked about duplicating data across systems that was error prone and keeping your data siloed in systems*].

**(SP/CI)** We embarked on a project together that would [Recap project milestones and purpose ...."*remove those siloes and integrate the data. We implemented (product, integration, etc.) in order to enable scale in your organization and reduce the amount of manual time people were doing (x,y,z)*].

---

### Fulfill Promises:

- I understand that we… [*recap back to the customer how we fulfilled the promises or if we encountered issues, how we solved the issues*].

---

### Bridge Statement → Collect Proofs:

**BRIDGE STATEMENT:** *I had a chance to sync with the project team and they shared [share positive feedback from the team about working with the Client].*

- *What has gone well from your perspective?*
- *What did you like best?*

#### Check for issues:

*Is there anything we need to talk about that hasn't met the expectations we set?*

> 🔴 **If there are unresolved issues, STOP. We are no longer doing a FF - we need to address their concerns.**

---

### Collect Proofs:

*(Preparation: Create a hypothesis of what kind of proof you'd like to get - create questions around your hypothesis)*

- *How have you seen progress in [business objective]?*

*"That's terrific, thank you for sharing that. So what I'm hearing is [proof statement]. Is that right?"*

- *That's wonderful to hear. Would you mind if I sent you a CSAT survey for you to complete for you to share those statements?*
- *A 5/5 review is really meaningful to our Consultants and to our relationship with Salesforce. Do you believe we have earned that? May I send you a survey?*

#### Referenceable: (options)

- "If we have a customer that has similar needs and is seeking a Customer reference, would you be willing to speak to them?"
- "Sometimes our prospective Clients are looking for a Customer reference to discuss their experience working with SOLVD. If they are in a similar industry or have similar implementation needs, would you be willing to serve as a reference for us?"

---

## Harvest Referrals:

### Start with internal referrals

*When we first engaged, we also talked about [x, y, and z] that we didn't end up doing as part of Phase 1. Your consultant also noted these backlog areas. Of those, what are your biggest priorities?*

*It is also very common for our Clients to want go-live and "stabilization" support.* (Discuss their appetite for support vs going straight into the next project).

*I'm not sure if you are aware, but we offer services around Salesforce, Integrations, and AI Consulting.* **What other departments might be interested in talking with me about these areas?**

---

### AI CONSULTING - FREE INTRO CALL

1. Let them know that we are building an AI practice with the goal of taking the pain out of adopting AI for our clients.

2. Ask questions to gauge interest (such as the following):
   - *What's the current state of AI at your company?*
   - *Have you included AI in any of your internal processes yet?*
   - *Do you have an established AI strategy?*

3. If appropriate, ask if they'd be willing to do an intro call. *Make it clear that we're in the early stages, and looking to gather some data about how we can be most helpful*

4. Find out the best person/people to talk to, and loop Evan in via an introduction email and he'll schedule a 30 minute intro call to interview them and introduce the strategic plan if applicable.

*Great, [set action items for continuing to support them going forward].*

---

### External referrals

**BRIDGE STATEMENT:** *Before we close up, may I ask you some questions that might assist us in continuing to grow our business?*

*While I am going to be asking about specific companies or names, please know that I will never use your name without your permission. Trust is critically important to us.*

**(Give/Get)** - *Also note that if you refer anyone to us that ends up signing up, we will give you 5 hours of non-bill time (per Client) as a token of appreciation.*

#### [SAMPLE QUESTIONS TO GET THEM TO THINK OF SPECIFIC NAMES]

- *What colleagues do you have that use Salesforce that may be looking for a partner?*
- *What colleagues do you have that are looking to solve some of the same challenges you were trying to solve?*
- *Who are 3 people you've worked with at other jobs that you admire?*
- *What Clients/relationships would you feel comfortable with us reaching out to?*
- *I have a list of companies that I'd love to do business with. Who do you know that works at any of these organizations?* (**note - you got this list off of looking at their LinkedIn and seeing that they're connected to these companies**)
- *What committees do you serve on? Who can you think of that might benefit from speaking with us about Salesforce or AI.*
- ADD MORE QUESTIONS HERE

**Last Question:** *In addition to the names or companies we've talked about, what other names have come to your mind?* (often, this is your golden referral).

#### Get Permission:
- *May I use your name when reaching out?*

---

## SNE:

*Thank you again for all of this info. Let's touch base again on [SNE].*
